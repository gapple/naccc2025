    The North American Cycle Courier Championships is coming back to Canada!! With the Pacific Ocean, iconic North Shore mountains, and booming cycling culture, NACCC 2025 in Vancouver will be one you don't want to miss. A week-long event, Vancouver NACCC will feature:

    • A 2 night camping trip to Golden Ears Park
    • Races by local fixedgear crit host YVRFixed
    • Alleycats from Tyrone Siglos who has up to 300 folks attend Riley's Rips, 45% of which identify as women, trans, or non-binary
    • Qualifiers and championship events by NACCC regular and former Vancouver courier Robert Doherty as featured on the cover on Loop Magazine

    The lower mainland's seamless transition of pavement to trail, rich diversity, and many hills will be a challenging but beautiful setting for NACCC 2025 that will have something for everyone.

    Camping

    Escape the hustle of city streets and join us for a 2-night camping adventure at Golden Ears Provincial Park, BC. A scenic 60km ride from Vancouver will Golden Ears is the opportunity to immerse yourself in stunning mountain views, serene lakes, and lush forests, with plenty of trails to explore. Connect with fellow couriers from across North America, and get the true experience of BCs rain forests. A great chance to recharge your battery and relax before taking on the week's events.
